VFS GROUPS WEBSITE - SIMPLE LOAN APPLICATION GUIDE

IMPORTANT: THERE ARE TWO DIFFERENT OPTIONS

Yes—this is the correct customer flow, 


Apply directly → complete application goes to Admin → Applications.
Request a call → name/mobile/service goes to Admin → Service enquiries.



1. ASK VFS GROUPS TO CALL ME

The small form shown on a service page asks only for your name and mobile number.
This is not a complete loan application.

After you submit it:
- Your request is saved securely.
- It appears under "Service enquiries" in the VFS admin panel.
- The VFS team can see your name, mobile number and selected service.
- The team will call you and change the request status to New, Scheduled, Completed or Cancelled.


2. APPLY NOW

Use this when you want to send a complete application.

You can apply for:
- Personal loan
- Business loan
- Home loan
- Loan against property
- New or used car loan
- Education loan
- Loan against shares
- Working capital finance
- Insurance services
- Investment services

How to apply:

1. Open the VFS Groups website.
2. Click the yellow "Apply now" button at the top.
3. Choose the loan or service you need.
4. Enter your personal details, including name, mobile, Gmail address, date of birth and location.
5. Enter your work and financial details.
6. Enter the amount you need and briefly explain your requirement.
7. Enter a referral code only if you have one. You can leave it empty.
8. Read and accept the consent statements.
9. Click "Submit application".

After submitting:
- The website gives you an application ID, such as VFS-APP-2026-000001.
- It also gives you a lead ID and shows the first status.
- Save or take a screenshot of the application ID.
- The application appears in the Applications section of the VFS admin panel.
- The VFS team checks whether the information is complete.
- This first submission is not a loan approval or loan offer.

How to track the application:

1. Click "Track application" on the website.
2. Enter your application ID and the same mobile number used in the form.
3. Enter the 6-digit verification code sent to your mobile.
4. You can then see the current status and status history.
5. If the VFS team asks for documents, you can upload PDF, JPG or PNG files from this secure tracking page.


SIMPLE EXAMPLE - APPLYING FOR A USED CAR LOAN

Ravi wants a used car loan of Rs. 5,00,000.

Option A - Ravi wants to speak first:
1. Ravi opens Services.
2. He opens "New & Used Car Loans".
3. He enters only his name and mobile number.
4. He clicks "Ask VFS Groups to call me".
5. VFS receives the callback request and calls Ravi.

Option B - Ravi wants to submit a complete application:
1. Ravi clicks "Apply now".
2. He chooses "New & Used Car Loans".
3. He enters his personal and location details.
4. He selects Salaried or Self-employed.
5. He enters his income, existing EMI and requested amount of Rs. 5,00,000.
6. He writes: "I need finance to buy a used car."
7. He accepts the consent statements and submits the application.
8. Ravi receives an application ID and saves it.
9. VFS sees the application in the admin panel and reviews it.
10. Ravi uses Track application with his application ID, mobile number and verification code.
11. If documents are requested, Ravi uploads them securely from the tracking page.
12. The relevant bank or NBFC makes the final approval and decides the interest rate and other loan terms.


THE TWO CUSTOMER OPTIONS NOW SHOWN ON EACH SERVICE PAGE

1. "Apply for this loan" or "Apply for this service"
   This opens the complete secure application with the selected service already chosen.
   The submitted details appear under Applications in the VFS admin panel.

2. "Request a call about this loan" or "Request a call about this service"
   This asks only for the customer's name and mobile number.
   The request appears under Service enquiries in the VFS admin panel.

After a callback request, the customer can choose:
- Wait for our call
- Complete application now

After a complete application is submitted, the website shows:
- Application submitted -> VFS review -> Documents, if requested -> Provider decision
- Download ID option
- Share on WhatsApp option
- Track application option
